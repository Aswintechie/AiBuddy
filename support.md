# AiBuddy Support

## What is AiBuddy?
AiBuddy is an AI-powered chatbot for Microsoft Teams that brings
conversational AI directly into your Teams workspace.

## Getting Started

### Installation
1. Open Microsoft Teams
2. Go to Apps → Search "AiBuddy"
3. Click "Add" to install the bot
4. Start chatting with AiBuddy in any chat

### How to Use AiBuddy
- **Personal chat:** Send a direct message to AiBuddy
- **Team channels:** @mention AiBuddy in any channel
- **Group chats:** Add AiBuddy to group conversations

### Available Commands
- Type `help` to see available commands
- Type `about` for information about AiBuddy
- Or simply ask any question naturally!

### Example Prompts
- "Summarize this for me: [your text]"
- "Help me write a professional email"
- "Explain [topic] in simple terms"
- "Give me 5 ideas for [your project]"
- "Translate this to Spanish: [your text]"

## Troubleshooting

### Bot Not Responding
**Problem:** AiBuddy doesn't reply to messages

**Solutions:**
1. Wait 30 seconds and try again (server may be waking up)
2. Check your internet connection
3. Try removing and re-adding the bot
4. Contact support if issue persists

### Slow Response
**Problem:** AiBuddy takes long to respond

**Solutions:**
- First message after inactivity may take 20-30 seconds
- Subsequent messages should be 2-5 seconds
- Complex queries may take longer

### Error Messages
- **"Service unavailable"** — Try again in 1 minute
- **"Rate limit exceeded"** — Wait 60 seconds
- **"Authentication failed"** — Reinstall the app

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is AiBuddy free to use?**
A: Yes, AiBuddy is currently free.

**Q: What AI technology powers AiBuddy?**
A: AiBuddy uses an AI model running on your own infrastructure.

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: Yes. AiBuddy is a self-hosted solution — all messages are processed
on your own server and the publisher collects no user data.
See our [Privacy Policy](privacy.html) for details.

**Q: Does AiBuddy work in Teams meetings?**
A: Currently, AiBuddy supports chat conversations only.

**Q: Can AiBuddy access my files?**
A: No. AiBuddy only processes text messages you send to it.

**Q: What languages does AiBuddy support?**
A: AiBuddy supports many languages including English, Spanish,
French, German, Hindi, Chinese, Japanese, and more.

**Q: Can I use AiBuddy in private channels?**
A: Yes, if your admin allows third-party apps in private channels.
