# Privacy Policy for AiBuddy

**Last Updated:** 28th Aprin 2026

## About AiBuddy
AiBuddy is a self-hosted Microsoft Teams bot that connects Teams users 
to an AI model running on the user's own infrastructure.

## Data Collection by Publisher
The publisher of AiBuddy does NOT collect, store, or process any 
personal information, messages, or user data. AiBuddy is a self-hosted 
solution—each user deploys the backend on their own server.

## Data Handled by Self-Hosted Deployment
When a user deploys AiBuddy on their own infrastructure:
- All messages are processed on the user's server
- AI processing happens through the user's chosen AI provider
- No data is transmitted to the AiBuddy publisher
- Users are responsible for their own data handling

## Microsoft Teams Data
AiBuddy uses Microsoft Teams Bot Framework. Data in transit is governed 
by Microsoft's privacy policy: https://privacy.microsoft.com/

## User Rights
Since the publisher collects no data, there is no data to delete or 
export. For data within your self-hosted instance, contact your 
organization's administrator.

## Third-Party Services
AiBuddy may interact with AI providers (e.g., OpenAI, Azure OpenAI) 
based on the user's configuration. Each provider has its own privacy 
policy.
