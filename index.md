## About AiBuddy

AiBuddy is an AI-powered chatbot for Microsoft Teams, bringing
the power of conversational AI into your team interactions.

### Key Features
- 🤖 Conversational AI powered by your chosen AI provider
- 💬 Works in personal chats, team channels, and group chats
- 🌍 Multi-language support
- 🔒 Privacy-focused — self-hosted with no publisher data collection
- ⚡ Fast and reliable

### Get Started

1. Find AiBuddy in the Microsoft Teams app store
2. Click "Add" to install
3. Start chatting!

For help, see our [Support page](support.html).
